A small poem for my love …

kaise batao mai tumhe mere liye tum kon


kaise batao mai tumhe mere liye tum kon ho. tum jindigi tum mandhegi.jevaan ka sangeet (song 🎼) ho tum

tum kwaab(dream 🛌) mai tum dhin(day 🌅) mai raat(night 🌃) mai tum kaise batao mai tumhe mere liye tum kon

kaise batao mai tumhe mere liye tum kon ho. tum isq💞 ho tumhi mohbhaat💝 ho

mereliye hasna(laugh 😂) bhi rona(cry 😭) bhi tum .mereliye tum shaam(evening 🌇) mai tum kaam mai mereliye panna(achive 🏆)bhi tum khona(lose 😞)bhi tum

kaise batao mai tumhe mere liye tum kon ho .

.mere janngna(wake up 😪) bhi tum ,mere liye sona(sleep 😴) bhi tum

jau kahi dheku kahi tum ho waha tum ho wahi(wherever 🛳 i go whatever i see you are there) tumare bheena tho mai kuch bhi nahi(without you nothing ️ )

kaise batao mai tum kitni khubsurat hoo

tumara roop 👧 ye jindhigi ki dhoop hai, tere chere(face 👧) mai hai chandhini (moon 🌝), aako (eyes 👁️) mai hai ragini(lovely godess 🧚),tapti(hot 🔥) badhan(body) hai tumari,teer(arrow ↦) jaisa jheeb(mouth 👄) hai aahhee!!! 💘

kaise batao mai tumhe mere liye tum kon ho

mereliye tum mere dharam(religion) ho puraab(east) mai bhi tum pasimm(west) mai bhi tum ,sare(entire) mere jevaan(life) mai tum ,mereliye rasta(road 🛣️) bhi tum mere liye manzil(goal 🏆) bhi tum

kaise batao mai tumhe mere liye tum kon

mai dhekta bass tum ko hum (i only see you),mai manta bass tumko hum (i only trust you),mai pyaar bass tumse hum (i only love you)

kaise batao mai tumhe mere liye tum kon ho

i really love you baby
